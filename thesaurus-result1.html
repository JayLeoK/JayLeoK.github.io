Genres/Roots: Rap, Hip-Hop, G-Funk, West Coast

"The Next Episode" is a single by American rapper Dr. Dre, and features Snoop Dogg, Kurupt, and Nate Dogg, the last being uncredited. It is a sequel to Dre's famous single from The Chronic, "Nuthin' but a 'G' Thang". The song peaked at number 23 on the Billboard Hot 100.
